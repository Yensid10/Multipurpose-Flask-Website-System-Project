Customers are at the fore-front of our business- our success is measured almost entirely by how they feel at the beginning, middle, and end of their meal. Due to this, it is very imporant that the first impression you make is one that is inviting and friendly. Don't view patrons as customers, but rather friends you would enjoy having a conversation with outside of work. More often than not, most customers will be more than happy to talk and respond to you with the same respect and friendliness you show them. However, there will always be bad apples every so often. It is important to still maintain a level of respect and tolerance with these types of customers, and to never bring yourself to their level, instead maintaining the level of service and friendliness Oaxaca is known for.

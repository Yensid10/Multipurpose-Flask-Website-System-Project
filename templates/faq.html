Although customers can edit out items specific to their own dietary restrictions, it is still good to learn the menu and help give recommendations based on their needs. Customers are at the fore-front of our business- our success is measured almost entirely by how they feel at the beginning, middle, and end of their meal. Due to this, it is very imporant that the first impression you make is one that is inviting and friendly. Don't view patrons as customers, but rather friends you would enjoy having a conversation with outside of work. More often than not, most customers will be more than happy to talk and respond to you with the same respect and friendliness you show them. However, there will always be bad apples every so often. It is important to still maintain a level of respect and tolerance with these types of customers, and to never bring yourself to their level, instead maintaining the level of service and friendliness Oaxaca is known for.

When a customer places an order, you will be able to see it on your device after signing in. New orders will appear on the left side of the screen in the orders tab, where the table number and order will come through, as well as an option to accept or cancel the order. There will also be a scrolling tab on the top right with more information about current orders. You can also edit orders after they have been accepted if you need to, and this information will be passed on to the kitchen staff so they can adjust the order appropriately.

You are able to send messages and updates about orders directly to the kitchen staff. These updates can be sent through your staff page in the form of either food notes (additional requirements of a dish) or direct messages. You can also go the old fashioned route of going directly to the kitchen to speak to the staff, if they are either too busy to respond to order changes, or if you need to adjust an order further.

Checking up on customers is a very important part of service that can not be ignored. This gives an opportunity to find out any grievances a patron had with their meal and how you can remedy it. This allows us to maintain a higher level of customer service by ensuring we do our best to make sure customers leave happy. You can also use this moment to offer drink refills, extra utensils and napkins, or anything else the customer needs.

If there is an issue which you cant resolve through this page, there are multiple routes you can take:

If it is a relatively small issue, such as a customer asking to edit an order after it has already been published and accepted, try talking to a fellow colleague, and getting their feedback and help on the issue.

If this still doesn't resolve the issue, or if the issue is too big, speak to your line/ shift manager to get their help in resolving the issue.

These situations can also be very important learning opportunities for you to learn how to manage and respond to different situations which you may not be used to.
